Bespoke Shipping Container Conversions

What’s ISOv8?

ISOv8® is a UK‐based specialist division of ContainerKing® Limited, focused on designing, manufacturing, and installing high-quality, bespoke shipping container conversions. Operating out of Scunthorpe, the company serves clients across England, Scotland, and Wales with both new and refurbished ISO containers.

Their customised solutions include container offices, workshops, canteens, gyms, meeting rooms, podcast studios, and modular classroom pods. Each unit can be fitted with insulation, acoustic treatments, LED lighting, luxury interiors, external cladding, and either single- or three-phase electricsâ€”tailored to the specific requirements of each client.

ISOv8® works with a wide range of customers, from SMEs and construction companies to schools, training centres, and blue-chip firms. Notable clients include ArcelorMittal, Exolum, and Mondi, reflecting the brand’s reputation for quality and reliability.

The company prides itself on expert consultation, rapid project turnaround, dependable UK‐wide delivery, and a strong commitment to providing smart, innovative, and durable portable building solutions.
